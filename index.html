Business Type Intelligence

Business Type Key Information to Collect Best Approach
Retail Shops (Clothes, Electronics) Inventory type, payment methods, delivery options, popular products Pose as customer asking about products and ordering process
Restaurants/Food Sellers Menu, delivery options, order methods, hours, location Ask about online ordering or reservations
Gadget/Equipment Sellers Product specs, stock availability, pricing, warranties Inquire about product details and after-sales support
Service Businesses (Salons, Plumbers) Booking system, pricing, working hours, customer inquiries Ask about appointment scheduling and availability
Cooperatives/Institutions Services offered, target members, registration or loan services Position as digital solution for member engagement
