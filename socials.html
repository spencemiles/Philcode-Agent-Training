Platform-Specific Scouting Strategies

f
Facebook

Best For:

  • Local small businesses
  • Community groups
  • Marketplace sellers
  • Service providers

Scouting Techniques:

  • Join local business groups (e.g., "Nairobi Small Businesses")
  • Search Marketplace for sellers without websites
  • Look for pages with poor design/no CTA buttons
  • Monitor comments for "Do you have a website?"

Top Search Terms:

"no website" "DM to order" "contact for price" "available on WhatsApp"
ig
Instagram

Best For:

  • Creative businesses
  • Fashion & beauty
  • Food businesses
  • Young entrepreneurs

Scouting Techniques:

  • Search location tags (e.g., #NairobiBusiness)
  • Look for "Link in bio" with no actual website
  • Find businesses with high engagement but no online store
  • Check Stories for "DM to order" posts

Top Search Hashtags:

#NairobiSalon #KenyanMade #MombasaBusiness #KisumuHustle
in
LinkedIn

Best For:

  • Professional services
  • B2B companies
  • Freelancers
  • Corporate clients

Scouting Techniques:

  • Search for "Founder" or "Owner" in job titles
  • Look for minimal company pages
  • Find posts about business growth challenges
  • Connect with startup founders

Top Search Terms:

"growing my business" "new startup" "digital presence" "business expansion"
tt
TikTok

Best For:

  • Young entrepreneurs
  • Trendy products
  • Creative services
  • Viral businesses

Scouting Techniques:

  • Search #SmallBusinessKenya
  • Look for "DM to order" in captions
  • Find businesses showing products but no store
  • Watch for comments asking about websites

Top Search Hashtags:

#KenyanBusiness #HustleKE #MadeInKenya #SupportLocalKE
wa
WhatsApp

Best For:

  • Local informal businesses
  • Service providers
  • Quick transactions
  • Community sellers

Scouting Techniques:

  • Join business WhatsApp groups
  • Monitor status updates for sellers
  • Look for "WhatsApp only" businesses
  • Find catalog sellers without websites

Key Indicators:

"Call to order" "WhatsApp only" "No website" "DM for price"
